# 📊 Sales Performance & Business Intelligence Dashboard
## Project Overview
This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales operations, tracking 23.6K order details and $30.1M in sub-total revenue. The project focuses on transforming raw transactional data into actionable insights regarding order lifecycles, product performance, and regional sales trends.

## 🛠️ Technical Skills DemonstratedData
. Modeling: Implemented a Star Schema to efficiently connect Fact tables with Dimension tables (Date, Territory, Product, and Status)
. Advanced DAX: Authored custom measures to calculate key business metrics including Total Due ($33.9M), Total Tax ($2.9M), and Freight Costs ($916K)
. Data Visualization: Utilized a variety of advanced visuals including Time-Series charts, Tree Maps for categorical breakdowns, and Dual-Axis charts for territory analysis. 

## 📈 Key Insights & FeaturesOrder Lifecycle Tracking: 
. Analyzed the distribution of 1.465K orders by status, revealing that Approved (27.03%), In Process (26.76%), and Shipped (25.53%) statuses account for the majority of the volume. 
. Trend Analysis: Identified a significant growth trajectory in order volume starting mid-2012, peaking in late 2013.  
Product Hierarchy: Optimized inventory insights by categorizing order details, showing Bikes (9.1K details) and Components (7.4K details) as the primary revenue drivers. 
. Geographical Performance: Developed a dual-metric analysis for territories, identifying Canada as the leading market by both order count and sub-total amount.

## 📂 Tools Used
Power BI Desktop: Dashboard design and report authoring. 
Power Query: Data transformation and cleaning. 
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Complex business logic and KPI calculations.
